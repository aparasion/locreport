XTM International has acquired Consoltec, the developer of FlowFit, a secure Translation Business Management System (TBMS) tailored for enterprise localization. This strategic move enhances XTM's position in the TBMS market by combining its existing solution, XTRF, with FlowFit’s capabilities, creating a comprehensive platform designed to address the operational challenges faced by localization teams in large organizations.

The acquisition is significant for the localization and language services industry as it underscores the growing demand for integrated solutions that streamline multilingual content management. With enterprises under pressure to deliver high-quality translations quickly and securely, the combined offerings of XTM and Consoltec promise to provide enhanced visibility, control, and compliance, particularly for regulated industries. This merger reflects a broader trend toward AI-driven automation and centralized management in localization workflows.

Localization professionals should note that this partnership aims to create a robust ecosystem that not only meets current market needs but also positions enterprises for future growth. The integration of FlowFit with XTM's technology could redefine how organizations approach their localization strategies, emphasizing the importance of agility and security in a rapidly evolving landscape.
