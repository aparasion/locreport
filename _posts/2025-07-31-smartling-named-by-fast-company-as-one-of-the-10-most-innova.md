Smartling, Inc. has been recognized by Fast Company as one of the World’s Most Innovative Companies for 2025 in the Business Services category. This accolade highlights Smartling's role in transforming the localization landscape, particularly for online retailers and global websites, through its advanced AI-driven translation solutions. The company’s innovations, including its AI Translation Toolkit, have significantly accelerated translation processes while ensuring high quality and cost efficiency.

This recognition underscores the growing importance of AI in the localization industry, as companies increasingly seek to streamline workflows and enhance translation accuracy. Smartling’s technology not only improves turnaround times but also supports major brands like Pinterest and Shopify in overcoming language barriers, which is crucial for global commerce.
