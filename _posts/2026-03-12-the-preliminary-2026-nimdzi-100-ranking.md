The 2026 Nimdzi 100 ranking has been updated to reflect the evolving landscape of the language services industry, now including both language service providers (LSPs) and technology solution companies. This annual ranking, based on self-reported revenues and extensive research, showcases the 100 largest language providers globally, highlighting a consistent growth trend even among the smallest players, with the 100th provider reporting $18.5 million in revenue for 2025.

This shift in ranking criteria underscores the impact of AI on the localization and translation sectors, revealing that traditional translation and localization services still dominate revenue streams, accounting for 68.4%. However, the slight dip in overall optimism from industry providers indicates a cautious outlook for 2026, with an average rating of 6.2 compared to 6.5 in 2025, suggesting a need for strategic adaptation amid market changes.
