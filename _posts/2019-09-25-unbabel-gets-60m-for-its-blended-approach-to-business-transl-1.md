Unbabel, the Lisbon-based startup known for its innovative translation platform that merges machine translation with human expertise, has successfully secured $60 million in a Series C funding round led by Point72 Ventures. This latest investment brings Unbabel's total funding to $91 million and highlights the growing demand for scalable multilingual customer service solutions. The company now boasts over 100,000 registered freelance translators, significantly expanding its capacity to meet the needs of enterprise clients like Facebook, Microsoft, and Booking.com.

This funding will enable Unbabel to enhance its AI capabilities and support its expansion in the US, Europe, and Asia. With claims of up to 76% cost savings compared to traditional translation methods, Unbabel is positioning itself as a key player in the localization industry, particularly in sectors such as travel, gaming, and e-commerce. 

For localization professionals, the implications are clear: the integration of AI with human translation is not just a trend but a transformative approach that can drive efficiency and customer satisfaction in global service delivery.
