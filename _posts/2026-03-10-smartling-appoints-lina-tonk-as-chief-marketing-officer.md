Smartling has appointed Lina Tonk as Chief Marketing Officer, bringing over 20 years of experience in marketing for high-growth software companies. In her new role, Tonk will spearhead Smartling's global marketing strategy, enhancing its position as a leader in AI-powered translation and driving demand for its innovative platform. Her previous successes at Recurly and isolved highlight her ability to build customer-centric teams and achieve commercial growth, making her a strategic asset for Smartling as it continues to expand.

This leadership change comes at a pivotal time for Smartling, which reported a remarkable 218% growth in AI and AI-human translation in 2025. As enterprises increasingly adopt AI for localization, Tonk's expertise will be crucial in communicating Smartling's value proposition and differentiating its offerings in a competitive market.
