In her Christmas message, Anu expresses gratitude for the ELIA community, highlighting the importance of connection and support throughout the year. She reflects on the success of four events held in 2025, which saw varying attendance levels but high Net Promoter Scores, indicating that members value in-person interactions despite economic challenges. Upcoming events include Together in Porto and Focus on Execs in Chania, along with Networking Days and Focus on PM later in the year.

Anu emphasizes ELIA’s commitment to providing ongoing value through various initiatives, such as the ELIA Roots interview series and industry insights. With the addition of 10 new members, ELIA now comprises 169 member companies. The next Annual General Meeting is scheduled for January 21, 2026, where members can share their perspectives and learn about future plans.

As the year concludes, Anu encourages collaboration and mutual support within the community, wishing everyone a joyful holiday season. For more details, readers are invited to explore the full article.
