Smartling, Inc. has unveiled major enhancements to its LanguageAITM platform, reinforcing its status as a leading AI-driven solution for enterprise translation and localization. Key updates include a new AI Translation feature designed to deliver MTPE-quality translations at half the cost and turnaround time, alongside a robust Language Quality Assurance (LQA) suite and advanced data visualization capabilities. CEO Bryan Murphy emphasized that these innovations empower clients to scale localization efforts without sacrificing quality.

These developments are significant for the localization and language services industry, as they reflect a growing trend towards leveraging AI to streamline workflows and reduce costs. The introduction of integrated quality assessments and the ability to export translation data for business intelligence purposes will enable enterprises to align localization efforts with broader company objectives, enhancing operational efficiency.
