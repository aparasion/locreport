OOONA has achieved the TPN Gold Star Shield, the highest level of content security accreditation from the Trusted Partner Network (TPN). This accolade signifies that OOONA has successfully addressed every Best Practice and Additional Recommendation required by TPN, reinforcing its commitment to security in the media localization industry. Additionally, OOONA received the TPN Stella Security Award, further highlighting its dedication to maintaining stringent security measures.

This development is crucial for the localization and language services sector as it sets a benchmark for content security standards. With increasing concerns over data breaches and content leaks, OOONA's recognition emphasizes the importance of robust security protocols in building client trust. Their integrated platform, built on AWS infrastructure, showcases a scalable solution that meets the demands of organizations handling sensitive media content.

For localization professionals, OOONA's achievement underscores the necessity of prioritizing security in workflows. As clients seek assurance regarding the protection of their assets, adherence to high security standards can differentiate service providers in a competitive market.
