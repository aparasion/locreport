TransPerfect Legal has announced two significant appointments aimed at enhancing its consulting and investigations capabilities in high-growth markets. Angie Nolet joins as Vice President of Consulting & Information Governance in Seattle, bringing over ten years of experience in legal and eDiscovery roles. Haleh Hamzavian takes on the role of Senior Director of Investigations in Dubai, with a strong background in white-collar crime and corporate investigations.

These strategic hires underscore TransPerfect's commitment to expanding its services in the legal technology landscape, particularly in regions with growing demand for compliance and investigation solutions. Nolet and Hamzavian's expertise will bolster the firm's ability to advise clients on complex data strategies and regulatory challenges, aligning with current trends in AI-driven legal workflows and the increasing need for robust governance frameworks.
