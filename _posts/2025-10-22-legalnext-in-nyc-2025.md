TransPerfect Legal is hosting LegalNEXT, a one-day conference tailored for legal professionals, featuring three specialized tracks: Antitrust, eDiscovery, and In-House Counsel. This event aims to facilitate knowledge sharing through expert-led sessions that focus on practical solutions rather than promotional content, encouraging meaningful discussions among attendees.

For localization and language services professionals, LegalNEXT presents an opportunity to gain actionable insights that can be directly applied to enhance workflows and processes within legal contexts. With over 200 legal professionals expected, the conference offers a unique environment for networking and building connections that could lead to collaborative opportunities in the legal localization space.

Attendees can earn CLE credits while engaging with leading experts who will share real-world experiences and strategies. If you’re looking to deepen your understanding of the intersection between legal practice and localization, I highly recommend checking out the full details of LegalNEXT for a chance to expand your professional network and expertise.
