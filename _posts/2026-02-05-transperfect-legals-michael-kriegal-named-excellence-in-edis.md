Michael Kriegal, Vice President of Consulting Services at TransPerfect Legal, has been named a finalist for the Excellence in eDiscovery Award at Cellebrite’s 2026 JUSTYS. This recognition highlights his leadership in navigating complex, large-scale eDiscovery challenges, particularly in managing multilingual datasets and integrating advanced technology solutions.

Kriegal's team has expanded significantly, now comprising over 20 specialists dedicated to high-volume eDiscovery tasks. Their expertise in handling diverse data sources and edge cases positions TransPerfect Legal as a frontrunner in the legal technology sector. The company’s commitment to innovation, including the development of AI-powered document review tools, underscores the growing importance of technology in legal workflows.
