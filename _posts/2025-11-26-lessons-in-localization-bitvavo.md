Bitvavo, a rapidly growing European fintech company, is transforming its localization strategy to enhance user experience across multiple markets. With a primary focus on English for its trading app, Bitvavo is expanding into key European locales, including Germany, France, and Spain, while managing content in six languages. Under the leadership of Emma Wallerstein, the Localization Program Manager, the company is evolving its localization efforts from a cost center to a strategic enabler for product and marketing teams.

This shift is crucial as Bitvavo navigates increasing localization demands driven by its expansion. By integrating localization across various departments and utilizing tools like Lokalise TMS and internal GPT models, Bitvavo aims to streamline workflows and improve efficiency. The anticipated doubling of localization spend and request volumes by 2025 underscores the growing importance of effective localization in supporting business objectives.
