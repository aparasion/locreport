LEO's 13th International Virtual Conference is set for June 25-26, 2026, inviting GALA members to engage with the theme “We Are Here To Stay: The Irreplaceable Language Industry.” This conference highlights the ongoing significance of interpreters, translators, and language professionals amid rapid technological advancements and AI integration in the field. 

For localization and language services professionals, this event presents a prime opportunity to enhance skills and stay informed about industry shifts. Attendees can expect expert-led sessions focused on AI, ethics, and practical strategies relevant to various sectors, including healthcare and legal interpreting. The conference also features networking opportunities and live Q&A sessions, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange among global peers.

A key takeaway is the emphasis on continuous professional development in an evolving landscape, underscoring the importance of adapting to new technologies and trends to maintain a competitive edge in the language services industry.
