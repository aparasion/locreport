Iyuno-SDI Group, a key player in media localization, has secured $160 million in funding from SoftBank Vision Fund 2, positioning it as one of the company's largest shareholders. This development follows the recent merger of Iyuno Media Group and SDI Media, enhancing their capabilities in providing translation services, including subtitles and dubbing, across a wide array of entertainment platforms.

The infusion of capital is significant for the localization industry, as it underscores the growing demand for scalable and efficient language services in the media sector. Iyuno-SDI Group's proprietary cloud-based ERP software and specialized neural machine translation engines are designed to streamline workflows, enabling faster turnaround times for clients like Netflix and HBO. With a presence in 34 countries and support for over 100 languages, the company is well-positioned to meet the increasing needs of global audiences.

For localization professionals, this funding highlights the importance of technological innovation and strategic partnerships in enhancing service delivery and expanding market reach.
