GLOBO is redefining language access by focusing not just on the organizations that pay for services, but also on the linguists and limited-English proficient (LEP) individuals they serve. Their model emphasizes empathy and customization, recognizing that effective language access solutions must be tailored to the unique needs of different industries and populations. By collaborating closely with clients, GLOBO creates scalable language access programs that yield measurable results.

This approach has significant implications for the localization and language services industry. By prioritizing the experiences of linguists and LEP patients, GLOBO enhances the quality of interpretation and communication. Their commitment to ongoing support and training for linguists ensures that they can navigate the complexities of language and culture effectively, ultimately improving patient outcomes for millions in the U.S.

For localization professionals, GLOBO’s model highlights the importance of considering all stakeholders in the language access ecosystem. This holistic perspective can lead to more effective solutions and stronger relationships across the board.
