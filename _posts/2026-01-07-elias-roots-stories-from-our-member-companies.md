In a recent interview celebrating ELIA's 20th anniversary, Svetla Marinova from Sofita reflected on her company's long-standing relationship with the association, which began in 2006. Marinova highlighted the value of collaboration and knowledge-sharing with European companies, emphasizing how ELIA has been instrumental in Sofita's growth during a critical period of market expansion.

Marinova shared memorable experiences from ELIA events, notably the 2014 gathering in Tuscany and the 2015 10th anniversary celebration in Lyon, which fostered deep connections and learning opportunities. She expressed appreciation for the sense of belonging within the ELIA community, noting that it has provided ongoing support and valuable partnerships that have contributed to Sofita's success.

Looking ahead, Marinova envisions ELIA continuing to play a vital role in the language industry, driving growth and creating new opportunities for its members.
