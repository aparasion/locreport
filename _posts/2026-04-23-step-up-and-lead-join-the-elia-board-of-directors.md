The European Language Industry Association (ELIA) is seeking candidates for three open Director positions on its Board of Directors. This is a prime opportunity for experienced professionals in the language industry to influence the direction of this non-profit organization, contributing to its programs and initiatives while collaborating with fellow industry leaders.

Joining the ELIA Board allows members to engage deeply with the language services sector, shaping strategies related to finance, policy, and events. Directors not only enhance their professional networks but also gain valuable leadership experience and skills through ongoing development opportunities. This role is particularly significant as it enables members to elevate their company’s visibility and impact within the industry.

For localization and language services professionals looking to make a difference, this is a chance to contribute to an influential organization while advancing personal and professional growth. Interested candidates should submit their applications soon to be part of this dynamic team.
