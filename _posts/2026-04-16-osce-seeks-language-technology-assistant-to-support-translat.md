The Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE) is seeking a part-time Language Technology Assistant in Vienna to enhance its Conference and Language Services operations. This role will involve coordinating translation workflows, managing terminology, and integrating language technology and AI-driven solutions within the organization.

This position highlights the growing importance of language technology in facilitating efficient translation processes. By focusing on the technical and organizational aspects of language workflows, the OSCE aims to streamline operations and improve collaboration between language assistants and external tech providers. The responsibilities also include reviewing and optimizing existing workflows, which could lead to significant advancements in productivity and accuracy in translation services.

For localization professionals, this opportunity underscores the increasing demand for expertise in language technology and AI, signaling a shift towards more tech-driven approaches in the industry. Engaging with such roles could provide valuable insights into best practices and innovations in language service operations.
