Smartling, Inc. has been awarded the “Machine Translation Innovation Award” at the 8th annual AI Breakthrough Awards, recognizing its significant advancements in AI-powered translation solutions. The company’s platform enables businesses to translate eight times more content within the same budget, delivering high-quality, on-brand translations more efficiently and cost-effectively than traditional methods. Smartling’s integration of various machine translation engines and its new AI Translation (AIT) feature allows for automated, near-instant translations without sacrificing quality, streamlining workflows for localization professionals.

This recognition underscores the growing importance of AI in the localization and language services industry, as companies seek faster and more reliable ways to connect with global audiences. Smartling’s offerings, which include access to leading LLMs and customizable tools, position it as a key player in enhancing translation quality while optimizing costs and turnaround times.

For localization professionals, the implication is clear: leveraging AI-driven solutions like Smartling’s can significantly enhance operational efficiency and scalability, allowing businesses to maintain a competitive edge in an increasingly globalized market.
