Pairaphrase has launched PairaphraseGPT, a custom generative AI engine tailored for translation and localization, now integrated into its AI Translation Management System. This innovative tool combines advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) with robust security measures, making it suitable for organizations needing to comply with regulations like HIPAA and GDPR. PairaphraseGPT allows users to translate entire files or generate original multilingual content in over 160 languages while maintaining formatting across 28 file types.

This development is significant for the localization industry as it enhances workflow efficiency and content quality. The platform offers flexibility with plug-and-play LLMs, domain and style controls, and an AI Sandbox for creative transcreation, all while ensuring data security and compliance. Such capabilities can streamline processes for enterprises, enabling them to meet diverse language needs more effectively.

For localization professionals, PairaphraseGPT represents a leap forward in AI-driven translation technology. I highly recommend checking out the full details of this exciting release at Pairaphrase’s website.
