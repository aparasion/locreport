A new four-part webinar series, co-hosted by the Canadian Language Industry Association (CLIA) and the European Language Industry Association (ELIA), aims to empower language service leaders with strategies for sustainable success amid industry changes. Kicking off on March 24, 2026, the series will feature experts like Dee Johnson, who will discuss how early business decisions can shape long-term value and exit options. The sessions will address critical topics such as marketing, talent management, and valuation, providing actionable insights tailored for professionals at various stages of their companies.

This initiative is particularly relevant as the language services market evolves, with buyers increasingly seeking partners that demonstrate clear business value beyond traditional service delivery. Attendees will learn to refine their marketing strategies, enhance client relationships, and adapt talent frameworks to meet changing demands.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the importance of intentional decision-making from the outset, which can significantly impact future growth and market positioning. Engaging in these discussions will equip leaders with the tools to navigate the complexities of the language industry effectively.
