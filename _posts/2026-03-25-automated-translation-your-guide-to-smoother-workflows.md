XTM has released a comprehensive guide on automated translation, addressing the challenges enterprise translation teams face amid rising content volumes and tighter budgets. The guide emphasizes the need for a hybrid approach that combines machine translation's speed with human oversight to maintain quality. By automating repetitive tasks, teams can focus on critical quality control, ensuring that translations meet enterprise standards without sacrificing efficiency.

The importance of this guide lies in its potential to transform localization workflows. With automated translation, organizations can achieve faster turnaround times, reduce operational costs by up to 30%, and scale their translation efforts effectively. The integration of AI-driven tools with human expertise allows for a streamlined process that enhances both speed and quality across various content types, from legal documents to marketing materials.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the necessity of implementing automated workflows that balance machine efficiency with human insight. By leveraging the right technology and integrating systems, teams can optimize their processes, ultimately leading to improved productivity and consistency in translation quality.
