XTM International has launched the Software Recorder, a groundbreaking tool that enables multilingual software video generation at scale, merging its Rigi and Video Creation Cloud products. This innovative capability allows enterprises to capture UI interactions once and automatically replicate them across localized versions, significantly reducing the time and costs associated with traditional video production processes. As Rob Finney, CMO of XTM, highlights, this solution addresses the challenge of keeping instructional videos current amidst frequent UI changes, a common pain point for many organizations.

The Software Recorder's intelligent UI capture process not only streamlines video creation but also integrates seamlessly with translation management systems to ensure consistency in terminology and language. This development reflects a growing trend in the localization industry towards automation and efficiency, enabling companies to produce high-quality, localized video content without the burden of repetitive recording.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is that adopting tools like Software Recorder can transform video production into a scalable and efficient process, allowing teams to focus on strategic initiatives while maintaining high-quality multilingual content.
