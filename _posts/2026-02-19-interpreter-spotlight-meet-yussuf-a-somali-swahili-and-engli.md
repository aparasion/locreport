Yussuf Poly, a freelance interpreter with over six years of experience, shares his journey and insights into the interpreting profession, emphasizing the importance of bridging language barriers in critical settings like healthcare and legal environments. He highlights the shift from face-to-face interactions to remote interpreting through phone and video calls, which demands new skills and heightened emotional awareness, particularly when dealing with vulnerable populations.

Poly's reflections underscore the emotional challenges interpreters face, such as the stress of high-stakes situations and the need for strong support systems in the industry. He also stresses that interpreting is more than just language translation; it requires ethical responsibility and a deep understanding of the emotional weight carried by clients' stories.
