Creative Words has announced a strategic partnership with Blackbird.io to automate multilingual workflows for enterprise clients. This collaboration combines Creative Words' localization expertise with Blackbird's content orchestration and governance technology, allowing organizations to streamline their multilingual content production and reduce manual processes. With over 200 integrations available, including major platforms like Adobe Experience Manager and Salesforce, businesses can create unified workflows that enhance efficiency in global content delivery.

This partnership highlights a significant trend toward orchestration in the localization industry, where the integration of content systems, AI technologies, and governance is becoming essential. By leveraging Blackbird’s capabilities, Creative Words aims to provide tailored automated workflows that align with each client’s localization strategy, ultimately transforming complex language workflows into efficient processes.
