Deniz Atalar, an accomplished interpreter with over five years of experience, shares her insights on the interpreting profession in a recent interview. She discusses her journey from studying translation to specializing in interpretation, highlighting the rewarding nature of her work, especially in medical settings where clear communication can significantly impact patient care.

Atalar emphasizes the importance of adaptability and continuous learning in the interpreting field, particularly as AI technologies evolve. She notes that while AI is changing demand and rates, human interpreters bring essential qualities—such as cultural understanding and contextual nuance—that machines cannot replicate. This perspective is critical for localization and language service professionals as they navigate the integration of AI tools into their workflows.
