Anna Fabres, a seasoned interpreter with over 20 years of experience, shares her insights on the evolving landscape of interpretation in a recent interview. As a member of the Chartered Institute of Linguists, Fabres highlights the unique challenges interpreters face, particularly in emotionally charged environments, and emphasizes the importance of maintaining professional boundaries while providing support.

Her reflections underscore the impact of AI on the interpretation profession, noting the need for linguists to diversify their skills amid rapid technological advancements. Fabres also calls for better collaboration between interpreters and legal professionals, advocating for training that fosters teamwork and mutual respect.
