The Globalization and Localization Association (GALA) is inviting industry professionals to participate in a survey titled "What's in a Name?" to redefine how the localization and translation industry describes its work. As the landscape evolves, particularly with the rise of AI, many practitioners feel that the term "localization" no longer captures the full scope of their services. This survey aims to gather diverse perspectives on what terminology best reflects the industry's current and future state.

This initiative is significant for localization and language services professionals as it highlights a collective need for a more accurate representation of the industry's expanding roles. The insights gathered will inform discussions at the upcoming WorldReady plenary session in Berlin and contribute to a public report, potentially influencing how services are marketed and understood in the broader market.

One key takeaway is the importance of participating in this dialogue; your input could shape the future identity of the localization profession and ensure it aligns with the evolving demands of clients and technology.
