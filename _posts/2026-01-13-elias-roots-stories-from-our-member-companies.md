Sotir Rangelov, a member of the European Language Industry Association (ELIA) since 2012, reflects on his experiences and the impact of the organization in a recent interview celebrating ELIA's 20th anniversary. Rangelov, who leads Oltrans, emphasizes the value of being part of a professional community that fosters learning and collaboration among language professionals. He recalls his first ELIA event, where he was welcomed despite initial concerns about language barriers, highlighting the supportive environment that encourages knowledge sharing.

Rangelov notes that ELIA has significantly influenced his business, particularly in areas such as marketing and workflow optimization. After attending an ELIA conference in 2018, he implemented a more efficient translation management system, reinforcing the importance of technology and quality assurance in achieving high standards. Looking to the future, he anticipates even stronger connections among members, with events focusing on critical industry challenges.
