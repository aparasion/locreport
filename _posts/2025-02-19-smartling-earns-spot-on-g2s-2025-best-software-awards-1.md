Smartling, Inc. has been recognized in G2’s 2025 Best Software Awards, securing the 47th spot on the Content Management Software Products list. This accolade highlights Smartling's commitment to transforming the translation of digital content, as determined by genuine user reviews on G2, the largest software marketplace that influences purchasing decisions for over 100 million buyers annually.

This recognition is significant for the localization and language services industry, as it underscores the growing importance of AI-driven solutions in streamlining translation workflows. Smartling’s LanguageAI™ platform is designed to reduce manual tasks, integrate seamlessly with existing technology stacks, and ensure high-quality translations at scale, which can lead to cost savings and faster turnaround times for enterprises.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the increasing value of user-driven feedback in software selection, emphasizing the need to stay informed about the tools that can enhance efficiency and effectiveness in translation management.
