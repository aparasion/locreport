Ben Faes, CEO of RWS, recently discussed the company's strategic direction and AI initiatives on SlatorPod, emphasizing RWS's role as a technology-driven partner for enterprises aiming to enhance global operations. He highlighted the importance of multilingual communication, intellectual property protection, and market insights in RWS's service offerings, positioning the company for sustainable growth in a rapidly evolving landscape.

Faes pointed out the swift pace of innovation and the democratization of AI, which allows organizations to create and implement solutions more efficiently than ever before. He stressed that the real potential lies in leveraging these advanced capabilities to address higher-value tasks, rather than getting bogged down in less impactful applications.

For localization and language services professionals, this conversation underscores the necessity of adapting to technological advancements and focusing on strategic applications of AI to drive meaningful business outcomes. Embracing these changes can enhance service offerings and improve competitive positioning in the market.
