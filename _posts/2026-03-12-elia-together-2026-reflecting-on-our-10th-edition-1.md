The ELIA Community convened on February 19-20, 2026, at the Crowne Plaza Porto for ELIA Together 2026, focusing on "The Power of Co-Creation: Collaboration on Focus." This two-day event brought together freelancers, language service providers (LSPs), vendor managers, and academics to explore how collaboration can help the language industry adapt to rapid changes. Attendees engaged in a variety of sessions that emphasized practical strategies for building partnerships and integrating AI responsibly into localization workflows.

The event's atmosphere fostered genuine connection, with participants sharing insights on collaboration frameworks, networking strategies, and the importance of adaptability in the face of technological advancements. Keynote speaker Sophie Solomon highlighted the significance of the Adaptability Quotient (AQ) in maintaining relevance within the industry. 

A key takeaway for localization professionals is the renewed emphasis on human connection and intentional collaboration, which can drive growth and innovation in an evolving landscape. The ELIA Together 2026 event not only explored these themes but also demonstrated their importance through meaningful interactions among participants.
