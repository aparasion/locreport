Smartling has achieved ISO 27001 certification, a significant milestone that underscores its commitment to information security management in the localization industry. This certification validates Smartling's robust measures for protecting customer data, which is crucial for enterprises in regulated sectors like healthcare and finance. As companies such as Verizon and Mastercard increasingly adopt Smartling’s AI-driven translation solutions, the importance of data security becomes paramount, ensuring that sensitive information remains protected throughout the translation process.

The ISO 27001 certification not only enhances Smartling's credibility but also aligns with the growing demand for stringent security protocols in enterprise RFPs. With this certification, Smartling can streamline security reviews, mitigating procurement challenges that often arise in vendor selection processes. This is particularly vital for organizations that handle sensitive data, as compliance with such standards is often a prerequisite for partnerships.

For localization professionals, Smartling's achievement highlights the necessity of integrating security compliance into AI workflows. As the industry evolves, ensuring data protection will be critical for maintaining client trust and facilitating smoother collaborations.
