XTM CEO Lorcan Malone recently highlighted the evolving landscape of localization, emphasizing the need for companies to view their operations as interconnected ecosystems rather than isolated translation tasks. As organizations grapple with increasing content volumes and diverse language requirements, Malone advocates for a shift towards orchestration, where tools and processes work seamlessly together to enhance efficiency and quality.

This approach is critical as consumer expectations rise for multilingual content, driven by advancements in AI and machine learning. Malone points out that successful localization teams leverage intelligent automation to streamline workflows while maintaining human oversight, ensuring that quality remains paramount. Companies that adopt this mindset can not only reduce costs but also accelerate time-to-market, a vital advantage in today’s fast-paced environment.
