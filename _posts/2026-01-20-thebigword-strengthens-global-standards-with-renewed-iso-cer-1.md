thebigword has successfully renewed six ISO certifications and achieved two new ones, solidifying its status as a leader in translation and interpreting services. The renewed certifications include ISO 9001 for Quality Management, ISO 14001 for Environmental Management, and ISO 27001 for Information Security, among others. These accolades are a testament to the company’s commitment to quality, security, and environmental responsibility, ensuring that clients receive reliable and high-quality language solutions.

For localization and language services professionals, these developments signal a growing emphasis on compliance with international standards, which can enhance operational workflows and client trust. The addition of ISO 18841 for Interpreting Services and ISO 20228 for Legal Interpreting further underscores the importance of ethical practices in specialized environments, particularly in legal contexts.

As thebigword continues to innovate, these certifications serve as a benchmark for quality and security in the industry, encouraging other companies to pursue similar standards to meet evolving client demands.
