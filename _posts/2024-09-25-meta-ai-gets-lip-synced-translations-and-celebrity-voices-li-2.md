Meta has unveiled a new voice feature for its AI assistant, Meta AI, during the Meta Connect 2024 conference. This update allows the assistant to respond audibly across platforms like Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and Facebook, using a selection of celebrity voices, including those of Awkwafina and Dame Judi Dench. While this feature resembles Google's Gemini Live, it lacks the emotive expressiveness of OpenAI's ChatGPT voice capabilities.

This development is significant for the localization and language services industry, as Meta is also piloting a translation tool for Instagram Reels. This tool aims to automatically translate and dub creators' speech while ensuring lip-sync accuracy, starting with select videos in English and Spanish. Such innovations highlight a growing trend in integrating AI-driven voice and translation technologies into social media platforms, potentially enhancing user engagement and accessibility.

Localization professionals should consider the implications of these advancements on content creation and audience reach, particularly as AI tools become more integrated into everyday communication.
