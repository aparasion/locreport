Ciklopea, a prominent European translation and localization provider, has recently achieved ISO 13485 certification, a critical quality standard for the medical device industry. This milestone, along with the upgrade to ISO 27001:2013—now encompassing software development—highlights Ciklopea’s commitment to quality and security in highly regulated sectors such as life sciences and technology. The company has also successfully maintained ISO 9001, ISO 17100, and ISO 18587 certifications, reinforcing its integrated management systems.

These certifications are significant for the localization and language services industry as they demonstrate Ciklopea's capability to support clients in navigating complex regulatory environments. The emphasis on quality, traceability, and risk management is essential for compliance in medical device and pharmaceutical documentation, where language accuracy is paramount.

For localization professionals, Ciklopea's achievements underline the importance of adhering to international standards, showcasing how robust quality management systems can enhance trust and operational excellence in delivering language solutions.
