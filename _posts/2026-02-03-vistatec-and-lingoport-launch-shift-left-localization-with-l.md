Vistatec and Lingoport have joined forces to revolutionize localization workflows by integrating Lingoport Localyzer with VistatecVerifier, enabling a shift-left approach that embeds localization into product design and development. This partnership addresses the common pitfalls of traditional, manual localization processes that often lead to delays and quality issues, allowing teams to localize user experiences faster and more efficiently.

The integration allows designers to see translations in real time within design tools like Figma, streamlining the workflow from design to production. This not only reduces manual handling and the potential for errors but also enhances collaboration among design, development, and localization teams. With VistatecVerifier's AI-powered quality assurance, organizations can maintain translation quality even as they accelerate their release cycles.

For localization professionals, this partnership signifies a critical evolution in how localization can be operationalized within product cycles. I encourage you to explore the full story to understand the implications for your workflows and how you can leverage these advancements.
