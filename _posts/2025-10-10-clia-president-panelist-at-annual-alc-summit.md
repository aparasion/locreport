Peter Madahian, President of the Canadian Language Industry Association (CLIA), recently participated in the Partner Association Panel at the ALC’s 23rd Annual Summit in New Orleans, where he discussed key strategies for member attraction and retention. He emphasized CLIA's commitment to providing value through events like WorldSpeak and year-round initiatives such as RENDEZ-VOUS, which foster community engagement and equip members with essential tools to navigate an evolving industry landscape.

Madahian also highlighted the importance of collaboration within the language services sector, noting CLIA's efforts to forge strategic partnerships with other associations. By focusing on meaningful conversations and joint initiatives, CLIA aims to advance shared goals and strengthen the overall language industry ecosystem.

For localization professionals, this underscores the critical need for ongoing engagement and collaboration in the industry, suggesting that active participation in community events and partnerships can enhance both individual and collective success.
