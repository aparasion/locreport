The Canadian Language Industry Association (CLIA) and the European Language Industry Association (ELIA) are launching a free four-part webinar series aimed at language service leaders and founders. Starting on March 24, 2026, the series will cover essential strategies for navigating business growth, marketing, talent management, and exit planning in the evolving language services landscape.

These webinars are vital for localization professionals as they address the pressing need for LSPs to adapt their business models and value propositions in response to market demands. Sessions will focus on how early strategic decisions can enhance long-term enterprise value, the importance of demonstrating tangible business impact to clients, and the necessity of evolving talent and quality frameworks in the age of AI. 

A key takeaway for attendees is the emphasis on intentional business structuring from day one, which can significantly influence future opportunities for growth, partnership, or acquisition, ultimately shaping the trajectory of their companies in a competitive market.
