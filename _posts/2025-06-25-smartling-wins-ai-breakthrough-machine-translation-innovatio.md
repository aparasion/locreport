Smartling, Inc. has been awarded the “Machine Translation Innovation Award” at the 8th annual AI Breakthrough Awards, recognizing its advancements in AI-powered translation solutions. The company’s technology enables businesses to translate content eight times faster while maintaining quality and brand consistency, all within existing budgets. Smartling’s platform integrates various machine translation engines and large language models (LLMs), optimizing workflows and enhancing translation efficiency.

This recognition highlights the growing importance of AI in the localization and language services industry. Smartling’s AI Hub and new AI Translation (AIT) offering streamline the translation process, allowing companies to deliver multilingual content quickly and effectively. By leveraging advanced algorithms, businesses can ensure high-quality outputs without the need for extensive human intervention, positioning themselves competitively in global markets.
