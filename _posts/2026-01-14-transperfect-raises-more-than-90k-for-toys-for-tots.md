TransPerfect has achieved a remarkable milestone by raising over $90,000 for the Marine Toys for Tots Foundation through its employee-led holiday campaign, marking the largest contribution in the initiative's 15-year history. This year’s fundraising effort saw participation from 27 offices across the U.S. and Canada, showcasing the collective spirit and generosity of the TransPerfect team, which began with just $700 in 2010.

This significant donation not only highlights TransPerfect's commitment to corporate social responsibility but also reflects a growing trend within the localization and language services industry where companies are increasingly engaging in community-driven initiatives. As businesses recognize the importance of giving back, such efforts can enhance employee morale and strengthen brand reputation, ultimately benefiting client relationships and market positioning.
