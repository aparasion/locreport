XTM is advocating for a transformative approach to localization through AI-orchestrated globalization, emphasizing the need for a shared intelligence layer across enterprise workflows. While AI has improved task-level speed, many organizations still struggle with fragmented systems that hinder collaboration and knowledge sharing. This lack of a unified foundation leads to inefficiencies and underutilization of AI's potential, as insights generated in one area remain siloed and unused elsewhere.

The introduction of an intelligence layer can revolutionize how AI operates within localization by connecting workflows, ensuring consistent application of policies, and providing real-time visibility into processes. This orchestration not only enhances efficiency but also mitigates risks associated with scaling AI, as organizations can track decisions and maintain quality standards across diverse content types and markets.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is clear: investing in an intelligence layer can unlock AI's full potential, enabling more cohesive and scalable globalization strategies that adapt to the complexities of modern enterprise needs.
