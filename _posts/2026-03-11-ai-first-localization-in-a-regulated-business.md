Deriv is revolutionizing multilingual content management by leveraging AI-driven automation in their localization workflows, as discussed in the latest episode of The Agile Localization Podcast. Host Stefan Huyghe speaks with Putri Kumala and Arshaad Mohiadeen, who share insights on managing over 20 languages while ensuring quality and compliance in a fast-paced environment. Their shift from traditional translation methods to automation has transformed their approach, allowing human teams to focus on strategic oversight rather than repetitive tasks.

This transition highlights a significant trend in the localization industry: the integration of AI not just as a tool, but as a partner in the workflow. Deriv’s methodology emphasizes maintaining quality through layered QA processes, ensuring that even automated translations meet high standards. By building redundancy and accountability into their systems, they tackle the complexities of multilingual content while adapting to market dynamics.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the importance of engineering trust in AI systems while retaining human oversight for quality assurance. I highly recommend checking out the full podcast episode for a deeper dive into their innovative strategies.
