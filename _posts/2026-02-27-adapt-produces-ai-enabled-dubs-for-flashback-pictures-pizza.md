Adapt, a localization company, has partnered with human linguists to provide AI-enhanced multilingual dubbing for Flashback Pictures' microdrama, "Pizza Guys vs Vampires." This collaboration utilizes advanced AI for transcription, translation, and voice generation, while ensuring that human linguists maintain the original tone, humor, and emotional nuances of the content. Matteo Natale, COO of Adapt, emphasized the importance of this human-in-the-loop approach in unlocking global distribution for emerging formats like microdrama. Mariko Nomura, Producer at Flashback Pictures, highlighted the necessity of global accessibility for content creators and the responsible use of AI in production. Flashback Pictures, known for its innovative approach to filmmaking, has plans for nine original feature films over the next three years, with "Pizza Guys vs Vampires" being its first release, achieving a rapid turnaround and securing a licensing deal in Latin America. Adapt aims to support studios and brands in scaling content globally with speed and cultural authenticity.
