Together 2026, scheduled for February 19-20 in Porto, aims to foster collaboration and networking among attendees through a series of interactive sessions and informal gatherings. The event emphasizes the importance of shared experiences, encouraging participants to engage in discussions over coffee, dinner, and city explorations.

Porto's excellent transport links, including a high-speed train from Lisbon and direct flights, make it easily accessible for attendees. The city’s walkable nature and vibrant atmosphere provide ample opportunities for networking outside the venue, with attractions like the Serralves Foundation, botanical gardens, and the Douro riverfront enhancing the experience.

Staying at the Crowne Plaza Porto, the event venue, is recommended to maximize networking opportunities. This allows for spontaneous interactions during meals and breaks, which are often where valuable partnerships and ideas emerge. Together 2026 is designed to create a space for meaningful connections and insights, ensuring attendees leave with strengthened relationships and a clearer vision for the year ahead.
