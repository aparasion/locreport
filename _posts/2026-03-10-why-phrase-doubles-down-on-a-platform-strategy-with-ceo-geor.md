Georg Ell, CEO of Phrase, returns to SlatorPod to discuss the evolution of the language technology platform amid the AI boom and changing enterprise SaaS dynamics. He emphasizes Phrase's commitment to an open ecosystem that empowers customers to build tailored solutions rather than relying on a closed system. This approach addresses investor concerns about long-term software value and reflects a broader industry debate on whether to build or buy AI solutions.

Ell highlights that while core AI translation quality may be plateauing, advancements in context handling, evaluation, and automated post-editing are enhancing translation workflows. He urges the localization industry to shift focus from mere cost reduction to driving business outcomes, such as improved hiring efficiency and revenue metrics. With predictions for 2026 pointing towards more sophisticated AI applications, Ell suggests that language technology will increasingly be viewed as a means of content adaptation and delivery.
