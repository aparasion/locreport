Ciklopea, a prominent European translation and localization provider, has achieved ISO 13485 certification, a significant milestone for companies in the medical device sector. This certification, alongside the upgraded ISO 27001:2013 for its Information Security Management System, underscores Ciklopea’s commitment to quality and compliance in highly regulated industries such as life sciences and technology. The company has also successfully passed audits for ISO 9001, ISO 17100, and ISO 18587, solidifying its position as a trusted partner for clients requiring stringent quality and security standards.

For localization professionals, these certifications highlight the increasing importance of compliance and risk management in translation services, particularly in sectors where regulatory documents and patient information are critical. Ciklopea’s integrated management system not only ensures quality and security but also enhances process transparency and continuous improvement.
