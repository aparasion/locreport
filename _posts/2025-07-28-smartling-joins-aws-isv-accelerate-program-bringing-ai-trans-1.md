Smartling has joined the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Independent Software Vendor (ISV) Accelerate Program, enhancing its visibility and accessibility for global enterprises. This co-sell initiative connects Smartling’s AI-driven localization platform directly with AWS’s extensive sales network, allowing customers to procure Smartling’s services seamlessly through their AWS accounts. The integration promises to streamline the translation process for websites, mobile apps, and product content across over 450 languages.

This partnership is significant for the localization and language services industry, as it reflects a growing trend towards cloud-based solutions that prioritize automation and efficiency. By leveraging AWS’s infrastructure, Smartling can offer faster turnaround times and improved translation quality, positioning itself as a leader in AI-powered localization. The collaboration also underscores the importance of strategic partnerships in driving innovation and meeting the evolving demands of enterprises modernizing their tech stacks.

For localization professionals, this development highlights the increasing necessity of integrating AI and cloud solutions into workflows, suggesting that adopting such technologies could enhance service offerings and improve client satisfaction.
