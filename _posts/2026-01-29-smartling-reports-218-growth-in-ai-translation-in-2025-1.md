Smartling has reported a remarkable 218% increase in AI and AI-human translation services in 2025, signaling a significant shift in enterprise adoption of AI technologies for localization. As companies transition from pilot programs to full-scale deployment, Smartling has enhanced its infrastructure to support this growth, introducing tools like the AI Hub for customizable AI provider selection and an AI Post-Editing Agent that ensures translation quality through automated checks.

This surge in AI-driven translation underscores a broader trend within the localization industry where enterprises are prioritizing integration of large language models (LLMs) into their workflows. Smartling's innovations, including automatic hallucination detection and seamless integration with existing developer environments, reflect a growing demand for scalable, efficient solutions that meet quality and budget requirements. 

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the necessity of adopting robust AI translation tools that streamline operations and enhance output quality, ultimately enabling organizations to scale their global reach more effectively.
