The Semmelweis Medical Linguistics Conference (SMLC 2026) is set to take place on June 5-6, 2026, in Budapest, Hungary, with an extended deadline for abstract submissions until March 30, 2026, and registration until April 31, 2026. This year's hybrid format allows for both in-person and online participation, making it accessible to a wider audience, including PhD students who can benefit from a discounted registration fee.

This conference serves as a vital platform for professionals in healthcare communication and medical linguistics, covering diverse topics such as medical translation, artificial intelligence, and patient-centered communication. The opportunity for participants to submit manuscripts for peer review post-conference enhances the potential for impactful contributions to the field.

For localization and language services professionals, SMLC 2026 represents a chance to engage with cutting-edge discussions and network with experts in medical linguistics. I encourage you to explore the full details and consider participating by visiting the conference website.
