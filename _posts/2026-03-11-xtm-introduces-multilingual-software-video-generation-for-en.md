XTM International has launched the Software Recorder, an innovative tool that revolutionizes multilingual software video generation by integrating its Rigi and Video Creation Cloud products. This new capability allows enterprises to capture UI interactions once and automatically replicate them across localized versions, significantly streamlining the video production process. With the rise of video content consumption—65% of global consumers now prefer video—this solution addresses the challenge of keeping instructional videos up-to-date with rapid UI changes.

The Software Recorder not only reduces the time spent on video creation by at least 60% but also lowers the overall cost of ownership for instructional videos. By automating the generation of localized videos with voiceovers, captions, and subtitles in over 100 languages, it transforms video production from a costly endeavor into a scalable content channel. 

For localization professionals, the implication is clear: integrating video creation with translation workflows can enhance efficiency and consistency across global content. I highly recommend exploring the full details of this development at the original source.
