Michael Kriegal, Vice President of Consulting Services at TransPerfect Legal, has been named a finalist for the Excellence in eDiscovery Award at Cellebrite’s 2026 JUSTYS (Digital Justice Awards). This recognition highlights Kriegal’s leadership in managing complex, large-scale eDiscovery matters, particularly in handling multilingual datasets and advanced technology solutions. Under his guidance, the consulting team has expanded significantly, reflecting TransPerfect Legal's commitment to innovation in the legal technology space.

This award underscores the growing importance of eDiscovery expertise in the localization and language services industry, especially as legal teams increasingly face the challenges of managing diverse and voluminous data. The recognition from Cellebrite not only elevates TransPerfect's profile but also signals a broader trend of integrating AI-driven solutions within legal workflows, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in data handling.

For localization professionals, the implications are clear: as legal technology evolves, there will be a rising demand for specialized language services that can navigate complex legal datasets, making it essential to stay ahead of these trends and invest in relevant capabilities.
