EasyTranslate is making waves in the translation services sector with its new generative AI-driven platform, HumanAI, which aims to revolutionize content translation. By pivoting from a human service-based model to an AI technology provider, EasyTranslate is leveraging large language models (LLMs) and short-term memory to enhance translation accuracy while significantly reducing costs and turnaround times. This shift is underscored by the company’s impressive track record, including its €3 million funding and a client roster featuring global brands like Wix and Monday.com.

This development highlights a critical trend in the localization industry: the integration of AI technologies to streamline workflows and improve efficiency. EasyTranslate’s approach combines traditional neural machine translation with customer-specific data, allowing for more nuanced translations that resonate with target audiences. The platform's ability to identify low-confidence translations and involve human editors only when necessary reflects a growing demand for high-quality, contextually relevant content.

Localization professionals should take note of EasyTranslate’s model, as it underscores the importance of blending human expertise with AI capabilities. This hybrid approach could redefine service offerings in the industry, emphasizing the need for continuous human involvement to ensure that translations not only meet grammatical standards but also align with cultural nuances and brand messaging.
