Meta has unveiled a new voice feature for its AI assistant, Meta AI, during the Meta Connect 2024 conference. This update allows the assistant to respond audibly across platforms like Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and Facebook, utilizing celebrity voice clones such as Awkwafina and Dame Judi Dench. While this feature is less expressive than OpenAI’s Advanced Voice Mode, it mirrors Google’s Gemini Live by transcribing speech and providing synthesized responses.

This development is significant for the localization and language services industry as it highlights a growing trend towards integrating voice technology into social media platforms. The introduction of a translation tool for Instagram Reels, which auto-dubs creators' speech and syncs lip movements, demonstrates Meta’s commitment to enhancing cross-linguistic engagement. This could lead to new opportunities for localization professionals to create content that resonates with diverse audiences.

Localization teams should keep an eye on Meta’s advancements, particularly the potential for voice translation in social media, as it may reshape how brands communicate in multilingual markets.
