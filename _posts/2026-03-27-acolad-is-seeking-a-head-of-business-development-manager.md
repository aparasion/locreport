Acolad is on the lookout for a Head of Business Development Manager to spearhead revenue growth and sales strategy in a hybrid role based in Boulogne, France, or Brussels, Belgium. This position is pivotal as Acolad continues to solidify its status as a global leader in content and language solutions, leveraging technology and localization expertise to help businesses expand across markets.

This role is crucial for the localization and language services industry, particularly as Acolad aims to enhance its market presence and tackle ambitious growth targets. The new Head of Business Development will be responsible for identifying new business opportunities, optimizing sales processes, and fostering client relationships, all while leading a motivated sales team. With a focus on enterprise clients, the position requires a strategic mindset and a deep understanding of market dynamics, especially in the localization sector.

For localization professionals, this development highlights the importance of strong leadership in driving business growth and innovation. The emphasis on strategic sales management and market expansion underscores the evolving landscape of the language services industry, where effective sales strategies can significantly impact overall success.
