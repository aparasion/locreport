FIFA is actively seeking to fill two language-related positions at its headquarters in Zurich and its subsidiary in Brazil, reflecting its commitment to enhancing language services ahead of major global events. The Zurich role is a full-time English translator position that encompasses a wide range of responsibilities, including translation, proofreading, subtitling, and terminology management, alongside providing linguistic support to internal teams.

This recruitment underscores the growing importance of language technology in the sports industry, particularly as FIFA aims to integrate AI-assisted translation and quality-control tools into its workflows. The emphasis on evaluating and optimizing AI tools highlights a trend where organizations are increasingly relying on technology to streamline language processes and improve efficiency.

For localization professionals, this development signals a crucial opportunity to engage with AI-driven initiatives and contribute to shaping best practices in responsible AI use within the language services sector.
