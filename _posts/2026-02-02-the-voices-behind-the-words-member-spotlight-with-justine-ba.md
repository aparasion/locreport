Justine Bastos, CEO of Loop Translations, emphasizes a people-first approach in her company, which is built on extensive project management experience. Loop Translations distinguishes itself by prioritizing client needs through proactive communication and transparency. The company also engages in social responsibility, donating a portion of its profits to local causes, including support for families in need and providing pro-bono services for NGOs and sensitive legal cases.

Bastos highlights the importance of maintaining work-life balance in a demanding industry, advocating for structured work hours while remaining available for urgent client needs. She believes that the language services industry is evolving, and companies that adapt to serve as integrators and advisors will thrive. Additionally, Loop is involved in supporting AI developments related to language recognition, showcasing its commitment to innovation in the field.
