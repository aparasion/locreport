The Think Global Awards has unveiled its 2026 honorees, celebrating significant contributions in leadership, language, and artificial intelligence. Damilola Ogunbiyi received the Think Global Leadership Award for her impactful work in promoting sustainable energy access worldwide. Katherine Melchior Ray was honored with the Think Global Language Award for her efforts in emphasizing the importance of cultural intelligence in international business. Intel earned the Think Global AI Impact Award for its advancements in responsible AI technology, particularly through innovations that enhance performance and accessibility in real-world applications.

These awards highlight the critical intersection of leadership, language, and technology in the localization and language services industry. As companies increasingly recognize the importance of cultural nuance and responsible technology, the honorees exemplify how strategic communication and innovative solutions can drive global success and sustainability.

Localization professionals should take note of these trends, as they underscore the need for a holistic approach that integrates cultural understanding and technological advancements to thrive in an interconnected market.
