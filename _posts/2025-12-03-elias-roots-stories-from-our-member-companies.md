In a recent interview celebrating ELIA's 20th anniversary, Tanya Karabasheva from arte.doc shared insights into her company's journey with the association over the past 13 years. Joining ELIA in March 2012, arte.doc sought to expand its connections within the European language industry and found a community that fosters collaboration and growth. Karabasheva highlighted the significance of the ELIA Together conference, which left a lasting impression due to its welcoming atmosphere and meaningful conversations.

The ELIA community has had a profound impact on arte.doc, providing a broader perspective on their work and encouraging innovative projects, such as their AI chatbot, Artina. Karabasheva emphasized the importance of ethical spaces for dialogue in light of industry changes like AI and automation, expressing hope that ELIA will continue to support diverse voices within the community. With arte.doc now represented on the Board of Directors, Karabasheva looks forward to contributing to the association's future. For more details, read the full article.
