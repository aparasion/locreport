IMAX is partnering with Dubai-based startup Camb.ai to leverage AI for scaling the localization of its original content, responding to the growing demand for non-English media. As the global entertainment industry expands to $2.8 trillion, with non-English content gaining traction even in traditionally English-speaking markets, this collaboration aims to enhance IMAX's reach and viewer engagement through efficient localization strategies.

The partnership highlights a significant shift in the localization landscape, where AI-driven solutions are becoming essential for companies looking to optimize translation workflows and reduce costs. Camb.ai’s innovative models, such as Boli for speech-to-text translation and Mars for speech emulation, promise to deliver nuanced and context-aware translations across 140 languages, including low-resource languages. This approach not only streamlines the localization process but also caters to diverse audience preferences in an increasingly globalized market.

For localization professionals, this development underscores the importance of integrating advanced AI technologies into workflows to remain competitive. As IMAX rolls out these AI translations, it sets a precedent for how entertainment companies can harness technology to enhance content accessibility and engagement on a global scale.
