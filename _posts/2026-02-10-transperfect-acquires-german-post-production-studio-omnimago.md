TransPerfect has expanded its media capabilities by acquiring German post-production studio Omnimago GmbH, a leader in film restoration and media accessibility services. This strategic move enhances TransPerfect Media’s studio network, allowing it to support filmmakers across Europe while maintaining Omnimago’s operations from its Ingelheim headquarters.

This acquisition is significant for the localization and language services industry as it strengthens TransPerfect's presence in the European market, particularly in high-demand areas like film restoration and accessibility. With Omnimago's expertise in nitrate film and archival materials, TransPerfect can now offer comprehensive restoration and post-production services from multiple locations, including France, Italy, and the US, thereby streamlining workflows for clients in the media and entertainment sectors.
