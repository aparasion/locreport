Julia Goncharik, Customer Relations Manager at World Global Eyes (WGE), emphasizes the company's unique approach to client partnerships through personalized collaboration and a hybrid human-AI workflow. Despite being a small translation firm, WGE has established a global presence, working with major technology companies and employing a culturally diverse team of professionals, including engineers and former language teachers.

WGE distinguishes itself by investing time in understanding clients' needs and business synergies, positioning itself as a growth partner rather than just a vendor. The company prides itself on agility and transparency, allowing for quick adaptations to workflows and open communication about technological advancements. Notably, WGE has also become a trusted partner in the legal field, handling high-profile criminal case translations while maintaining strict confidentiality.
