The International Criminal Court (ICC) is seeking a Language Services Manager to oversee its multilingual operations, which support hearings and investigations in various languages. This role encompasses the recruitment and quality monitoring of interpreters and translators, ensuring terminology consistency, and developing operational guidelines. The successful candidate will coordinate with internal teams and stakeholders while advising senior management on language-related issues.

This position highlights the growing demand for specialized language services in international legal contexts, emphasizing the need for tailored solutions that include freelance professionals and dedicated terminology resources. With the ICC operating in diverse linguistic environments, the role underscores the importance of maintaining high standards in translation and interpretation, particularly in legal settings.

For localization professionals, this opportunity signals a trend towards integrating advanced language technologies and strategic management practices in international organizations, reinforcing the critical role of language services in global justice initiatives.
