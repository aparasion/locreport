TransPerfect has reported a 7% increase in billed revenues for 2025, reaching $1.32 billion, marking the company's 33rd consecutive year of growth. This success stems from a blend of organic sales, strategic acquisitions—including industry leaders like Unbabel and The Mill—and a surge in customer adoption of its technology products, particularly its GlobalLink platform.

This growth highlights significant trends in the localization and language services industry, particularly the increasing integration of AI and automation into translation workflows. With GlobalLink technology licensing revenue rising by 18% and over 150,000 users of GlobalLink NOW, TransPerfect is demonstrating how businesses can leverage AI to streamline operations while maintaining quality and compliance. The expansion into new markets, such as the opening of an office in Riyadh, further underscores their commitment to global reach and service diversification.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the importance of embracing technology to enhance service offerings and operational efficiency. I recommend exploring the full article for deeper insights into TransPerfect's strategic moves and their implications for the industry.
