TransPerfect has acquired Omnimago GmbH, a prominent German post-production studio specializing in film restoration and media accessibility services. This acquisition enhances TransPerfect Media’s capabilities in Europe, allowing the company to offer comprehensive film scanning and restoration services from facilities across multiple countries, including Germany, France, and Italy.

The integration of Omnimago into TransPerfect Media is significant for the localization and language services industry as it expands the company’s footprint in the European media landscape. By leveraging Omnimago’s expertise in high-end post-production and accessibility, TransPerfect can provide more robust support to filmmakers and cultural institutions, addressing the growing demand for quality media localization and preservation services.

For localization professionals, this acquisition underscores the importance of partnerships that enhance service offerings and expand geographic reach.
