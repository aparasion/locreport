Palabra AI, an innovative startup in the AI translation space, has made headlines this April 2026 with the launch of its cutting-edge "streaming-native" text-to-speech (TTS) engine and the appointment of Andrey Feldman as CTO. Founded in 2023 and based in London, Palabra specializes in low-latency voice translation, boasting capabilities in over 60 languages with impressive performance metrics of less than one second latency and 99% accuracy.

This development is significant for the localization and language services industry as it underscores the growing demand for real-time translation solutions in voice applications. With Palabra's recent funding of USD 8.4 million aimed at enhancing their streaming-prediction model, the company is positioning itself to handle increasing usage, targeting 100,000 minutes per month and aiming for 1 million by the end of 2026. 

For localization professionals, Palabra's advancements highlight the importance of integrating AI-driven technologies to meet evolving market needs and improve efficiency in language workflows.
