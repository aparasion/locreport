TransPerfect's MPC Paris studio has taken the lead in VFX and picture post-production for Netflix's new series, Néro the Assassin, showcasing its capabilities as a major player in the visual effects industry. The studio delivered 238 VFX shots and managed the complete post-production process for all eight episodes, collaborating closely with French partners to create a visually cohesive narrative.
