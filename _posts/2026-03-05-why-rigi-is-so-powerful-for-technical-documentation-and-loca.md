Rigi is transforming the software localization landscape by bridging the gap between development and localization teams, offering a shared visual context that streamlines the entire workflow. This platform captures HTML previews of applications and links them directly to corresponding strings, allowing technical writers, translators, and in-country reviewers to see the exact context in which text appears, thus eliminating the common friction points that slow down the localization process.

The implications for the localization industry are significant. By reducing context-related queries by up to 60% and shortening localization turnaround times by 25%, Rigi not only enhances efficiency but also fosters better collaboration between developers and localization professionals. This alignment minimizes the risk of costly errors and delays in product releases, which are crucial for enterprises operating in multiple languages.
