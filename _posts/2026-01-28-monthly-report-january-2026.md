# ELIA Monthly Industry Report - January 2026

January 2026 marks a month of reflection and preparation within the European Language Industry Association (ELIA), as member companies celebrate their histories and look forward to upcoming events that foster collaboration and networking.

## Key Themes

This month, the focus has been on the significance of community within the language industry, as evidenced by personal stories shared by long-standing members of ELIA. The narratives highlight the evolution of member companies and the role of ELIA in supporting their growth. Additionally, anticipation builds for the upcoming Together 2026 event, which aims to enhance networking opportunities among industry professionals.

## Notable Developments

ELIA's 20th anniversary celebrations have prompted members to share their experiences and the impact of the association on their businesses. Svetla Marinova from Sofita and Sotir Rangelov, a member since 2012, both reflected on how ELIA has facilitated their companies' development and fostered a sense of belonging within the industry. These testimonials underscore the value of long-term relationships and the collective strength of the ELIA community.

In another highlight, Ivana Bojčić, CEO of BoLingo, shared her journey from a small startup to a recognized boutique language company. Her story exemplifies the entrepreneurial spirit prevalent among ELIA members and the potential for growth within the industry.

## Business and Market Signals

The upcoming Together 2026 event, scheduled for February 19-20 in Porto, is expected to be a pivotal moment for industry professionals. The event will include interactive sessions designed to encourage collaboration and networking, which are critical for business development in the language services sector. This focus on engagement suggests a trend towards more community-oriented approaches in the industry, as companies seek to leverage shared knowledge and resources.

## What to Watch Next Month

As the Together 2026 event approaches, attention will be on the outcomes of the networking sessions and workshops. The effectiveness of these interactions in fostering new partnerships and collaborations will be a key indicator of the industry's direction in 2026. Additionally, further member stories and insights are anticipated as ELIA continues to celebrate its anniversary, providing a deeper understanding of the evolving landscape within the language industry.
