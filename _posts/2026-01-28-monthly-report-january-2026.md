# ELIA Monthly Industry Report - January 2026

January 2026 marks a month of reflection and anticipation for the European Language Industry Association (ELIA) as it celebrates its 20th anniversary while gearing up for the upcoming Together 2026 event. This dual focus on heritage and future collaboration highlights the organization's commitment to fostering relationships within the language industry.

## Key Themes

The key themes emerging this month center around the importance of community and collaboration within the language industry. Member testimonials underscore the value of long-standing relationships with ELIA, illustrating how these connections have fostered growth and innovation. The upcoming Together 2026 event is positioned as a pivotal opportunity for networking and collaboration, further emphasizing the theme of unity within the industry.

## Notable Developments

Several member companies have shared their experiences and insights in interviews celebrating ELIA's anniversary. Svetla Marinova from Sofita highlighted the enduring partnership with ELIA, reflecting on how the organization has supported her company over the years. Similarly, Sotir Rangelov, a member since 2012, discussed the positive impact of ELIA on his professional journey, showcasing the association's role in enhancing member engagement and industry standards.

Her story exemplifies the entrepreneurial spirit prevalent among ELIA members and the supportive environment fostered by the association.
