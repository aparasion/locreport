On March 13, 2026, EasyTranslate announced the acquisition of Translated By Us (TBU), a Danish Language Solutions Integrator, marking a significant move in its strategy to modernize traditional service providers through AI. This acquisition follows EasyTranslate's previous purchase of World Translation in September 2024 and is aimed at enhancing operational efficiency via its centralized AI SaaS platform. With backing from Danske Bank Growth and the Export and Investment Fund of Denmark, this deal positions EasyTranslate for broader European market expansion.

The integration of TBU into EasyTranslate’s LangOps infrastructure will transform production workflows by leveraging automation and AI, shifting staff roles from manual execution to high-level linguistic oversight. This change is particularly relevant for TBU’s public sector clients, where compliance with strict data privacy regulations is crucial. EasyTranslate’s planned “HumanAI On-Prem” solution will further cater to these needs by allowing clients to manage AI translation tools within their own secure environments.

Localization professionals should note that this acquisition exemplifies a growing trend of integrating AI technology into traditional language services, potentially reshaping workflows and client interactions across the industry.
