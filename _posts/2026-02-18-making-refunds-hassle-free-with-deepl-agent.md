DeepL has launched DeepL Agent, an AI-driven tool designed to streamline the customer service process for handling refunds, returns, and exchanges. Unlike traditional automation that often requires complex integrations, DeepL Agent operates through a browser interface, allowing it to navigate various systems without the need for coding or IT support. This capability enables customer service teams to execute tasks end-to-end, significantly reducing the time and effort involved in processing support requests.

The introduction of DeepL Agent is particularly relevant for localization and language services professionals, as it addresses common pain points in customer service workflows. By automating the retrieval of information and processing of requests, the tool alleviates the burden on support teams, allowing them to manage higher ticket volumes without increasing headcount. This not only enhances operational efficiency but also improves customer experience by ensuring faster resolutions.

For localization teams, the key takeaway is the potential for AI agents like DeepL Agent to transform customer service operations. By automating routine tasks, teams can focus on delivering personalized support, ultimately fostering greater customer loyalty and retention.
