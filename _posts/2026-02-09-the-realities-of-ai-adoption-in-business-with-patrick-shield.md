Patrick Shields, an executive and PhD researcher in AI, joins Simon Hodgkins on VistaTalks Episode 188 to discuss the essential role of leadership in the responsible adoption of AI, particularly for SMEs. Their conversation explores the disconnect between the rapid availability of AI tools and the organizational challenges that hinder effective usage, emphasizing that the issues are rooted in culture and behavior rather than technology.

Shields highlights the prevalence of "shadow AI," where employees use AI tools without proper oversight, exposing businesses to various risks. He argues that successful AI integration requires a shift in leadership style from command-and-control to stewardship, fostering clear communication and engagement among teams. By treating AI as a workflow change rather than just a tool, organizations can redesign processes to maximize value while maintaining quality and accountability.
