ELIA is hosting its next Coffee Break session on April 2, 2023, from 13:00 to 14:00 CET, led by Managing Director John Terninko. This monthly interactive gathering offers language services professionals a chance to engage with peers, share insights on industry developments, and discuss emerging challenges in an informal setting.

These sessions serve as a vital touchpoint for members, fostering continuous learning and collaboration within the localization community. By connecting professionals from various sectors, the Coffee Break helps maintain momentum between larger ELIA events, ensuring that members stay aligned with the latest trends and best practices.

For localization professionals, participating in these gatherings can enhance networking opportunities and provide valuable perspectives that can inform business strategies and workflows. Mark your calendars to join this engaging forum and strengthen your connections within the ELIA community.
