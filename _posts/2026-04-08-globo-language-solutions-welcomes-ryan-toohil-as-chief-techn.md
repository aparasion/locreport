GLOBO Language Solutions has appointed Ryan Toohil as its new Chief Technology Officer, a strategic move aimed at bolstering its leadership and driving technological innovation. Toohil, who brings over 20 years of experience in scaling technology organizations, will play a crucial role in advancing GLOBO's AI-driven interpreting capabilities and enhancing language access solutions for limited-English-speaking populations.

This appointment highlights the growing importance of technology in the localization and language services industry. As companies increasingly integrate AI into their workflows, Toohil's expertise in platform development and strategic growth will be vital for GLOBO as it seeks to maintain its competitive edge. With a robust global network of linguists and a reputation for high-quality services, GLOBO is poised to leverage technological advancements to expand its reach across diverse sectors, including healthcare and education.

For localization professionals, Toohil’s leadership represents an opportunity to observe how AI and technology can reshape language access services, potentially setting new standards in the industry.
