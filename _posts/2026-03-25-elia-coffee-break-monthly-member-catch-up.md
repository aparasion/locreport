ELIA is hosting a Coffee Break session on April 2 from 13:00 to 14:00 CET, led by Managing Director John Terninko. This informal gathering invites members of the language services sector to engage in real-time discussions, share insights, and explore current industry trends. It serves as a platform for networking, allowing professionals to connect and exchange experiences in a relaxed setting.

These monthly meetings are designed to foster community ties and support continuous learning among localization professionals. They provide a valuable touchpoint between larger ELIA events, ensuring that members remain aligned with emerging challenges and opportunities in the industry. 

For localization professionals, participating in these sessions can enhance your understanding of market dynamics and help build meaningful relationships within the community. Engaging in peer-to-peer discussions can inspire new ideas and approaches to navigating the evolving landscape of language services.
