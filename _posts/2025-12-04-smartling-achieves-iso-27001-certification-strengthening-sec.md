Smartling has achieved ISO 27001 certification, a significant milestone that underscores its commitment to information security management systems. This certification validates Smartling's robust measures for protecting customer data, particularly as enterprises like Verizon, Mastercard, and Pinterest increasingly rely on its AI-driven translation services. The rigorous two-stage audit assessed Smartling's security protocols, including access management and incident response, ensuring that sensitive information remains secure throughout the translation process.

This development is crucial for the localization and language services industry, especially for businesses operating in regulated sectors such as healthcare and finance. With ISO 27001 certification, Smartling enhances its credibility as a trusted partner, addressing the stringent security requirements often outlined in enterprise RFPs. The certification not only streamlines vendor onboarding but also alleviates procurement concerns related to data security, enabling faster decision-making for enterprises adopting AI solutions.
