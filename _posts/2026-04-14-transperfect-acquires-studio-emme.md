TransPerfect has acquired Studio Emme SpA, a prominent Rome-based post-production and dubbing studio, enhancing its media division's global presence. This acquisition allows TransPerfect Media to leverage Studio Emme's 40 years of expertise in the Italian entertainment industry, while the studio's leadership will remain intact to ensure continuity and growth.

This move is significant for the localization and language services industry as it underscores the increasing importance of integrated media solutions that combine localization with high-quality audiovisual production. By incorporating Studio Emme's capabilities, TransPerfect can offer clients a more comprehensive suite of services, including dubbing, subtitling, and post-production, all while utilizing advanced technology and maintaining stringent content security standards.

For localization professionals, this acquisition highlights the trend toward consolidation in the industry, where companies seek to enhance their service offerings and global reach. It emphasizes the need for agility in adapting to evolving client demands in the media and entertainment sectors.
