GLOBO Language Solutions has made a significant mark in the localization and language services sector by ranking No. 34 on Inc.'s sixth annual Regionals: Northeast list, highlighting its impressive 147% growth over two years. This recognition places GLOBO among the fastest-growing privately held companies in the Northeast, particularly within the healthcare and medical industry, showcasing its commitment to quality and innovation in language access solutions.

This achievement underscores the increasing demand for effective communication in healthcare, especially for patients with limited English proficiency. GLOBO's technology-driven approach, which includes on-demand video and audio interpreting, AI-powered tools, and a vast network of linguists, positions it as a key player in enhancing patient experiences and addressing capacity constraints in healthcare settings. The company’s focus on cultural responsiveness and customer partnerships reflects broader trends in the localization industry where technology and human expertise converge to improve service delivery.

For localization professionals, GLOBO's success serves as a reminder of the critical role that language access plays in healthcare and the potential for growth in this sector. As the industry evolves, there is a clear opportunity to leverage technology to meet the diverse needs of clients while fostering better communication outcomes.
