XTM International has announced its acquisition of Consoltec, the Montreal-based company behind FlowFit, a secure translation project management system (TBMS) tailored for large enterprises. This move solidifies XTM's leadership in the TBMS sector by integrating FlowFit with its existing solutions, XTRF and others, to provide a comprehensive platform that enhances operational efficiency and visibility for language service providers (LSPs) and enterprises alike.

This acquisition is significant as it addresses the growing demand for rapid, high-quality multilingual content production in secure environments. By combining Consoltec's expertise in project management with XTM's AI-driven localization technology, the new unified platform promises to streamline workflows and improve data security, which is crucial for organizations operating in regulated sectors such as pharmaceuticals and finance.

For localization professionals, this development highlights the increasing importance of integrated solutions that can adapt to complex operational needs while ensuring compliance and security. The synergy between XTM and Consoltec could set a new standard for TBMS offerings, making it essential for LSPs to stay informed about these advancements to remain competitive.
