Creative Words has partnered with Webvisibility, an Italian agency specializing in digital visibility and AI-powered search optimization, to enhance multilingual GEO strategies. This collaboration aims to address the evolving landscape of AI-driven search, where traditional SEO practices are no longer sufficient. As AI platforms increasingly influence how consumers search for information and make decisions, businesses must adapt their online visibility strategies to ensure accurate representation across various languages and markets.

The partnership combines Webvisibility’s expertise in AI Search and Generative Engine Optimization with Creative Words’ localization capabilities, providing brands with essential support in adapting content for multilingual AI search visibility, optimizing their presence on AI-powered search engines, and developing AI-ready content strategies. This strategic alliance highlights the growing importance of multilingual visibility in the digital marketing landscape.

For localization professionals, this partnership underscores the necessity of integrating AI considerations into content strategies, ensuring that brands remain competitive in an increasingly complex digital environment.
