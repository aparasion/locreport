By leveraging AI-driven workflows, Wordly is positioned to address the complexities of multilingual communication in a global event, setting a precedent for future collaborations in similar contexts.

As events like the Olympics require seamless communication across diverse languages, the emphasis on real-time translation could drive further investment and innovation in the language services sector.
