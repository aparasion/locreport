Smartling has been recognized as the No. 3 company in Business Services on Fast Company’s 2026 list of the World’s Most Innovative Companies, highlighting its significant advancements in AI-driven translation. This recognition underscores the rapid adoption of Smartling's LanguageAI™ platform, which has enabled enterprises to scale their translation efforts efficiently while reducing costs and turnaround times dramatically. In 2025, Smartling reported a 218% growth in AI translation as companies transitioned from pilot projects to full-scale deployments.

The implications for the localization and language services industry are profound. Smartling's innovations, including new AI agents for error-checking and quality review, have streamlined workflows that traditionally slowed down translation processes. Clients have experienced cost reductions of up to 60% and improved turnaround times by six times compared to conventional methods. 

As Smartling prepares to launch its Linguistic Quality Assurance Agent in Q2, localization teams can expect even greater efficiency and quality assurance in their AI translation workflows, making it essential for professionals to stay informed about these advancements.
