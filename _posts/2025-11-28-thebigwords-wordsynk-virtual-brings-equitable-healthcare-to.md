thebigword has launched WordSynk Virtual, an innovative interpreting platform designed to eliminate language barriers in healthcare. This solution provides instant access to professional interpreters across over 250 languages, including American Sign Language, ensuring that patients with limited English proficiency can receive timely and effective care. Since its introduction, the platform has facilitated more than 224,000 multilingual interactions, significantly enhancing communication between healthcare providers and diverse patient populations.

This development is crucial for the localization and language services industry, as it highlights the growing demand for accessible language solutions in critical sectors like healthcare. By leveraging technology to connect patients with interpreters quickly—often in under 25 seconds for Spanish—thebigword is setting a new standard for inclusive care, ultimately reducing health disparities and fostering trust in healthcare systems.
