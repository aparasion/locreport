Deniz Atalar, an accomplished interpreter with over five years of experience, shares insights from her career in a recent interview, emphasizing the unique challenges and rewards of the profession. Specializing in Turkish and English, Atalar highlights the importance of cultural nuances and the need for continuous learning, especially in high-stakes environments like medical interpretation, where clear communication can significantly impact patient outcomes.

Her reflections underscore a vital trend in the localization and language services industry: the growing influence of AI on interpreting roles. While AI technologies are evolving rapidly, Atalar asserts that human interpreters bring irreplaceable skills to the table, such as understanding context and cultural subtleties. This distinction is crucial as the industry adapts to technological advancements while maintaining the integrity of human interpretation.

For professionals in the localization field, Atalar's experience serves as a reminder of the importance of ethical practices and the need to embrace AI as a supportive tool rather than a replacement. Understanding these dynamics can help linguists navigate their careers effectively in an evolving landscape.
