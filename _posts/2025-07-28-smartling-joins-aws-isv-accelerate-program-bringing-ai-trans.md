Smartling has joined the Amazon Web Services (AWS) Independent Software Vendor (ISV) Accelerate Program, enhancing its reach to global enterprises looking to streamline their localization processes. This partnership allows Smartling to integrate its AI-driven translation platform directly with AWS, enabling customers to access its services through their AWS accounts and facilitating faster localization across over 450 languages.

This development is significant for the localization and language services industry as it underscores the growing trend of cloud-based solutions in translation workflows. By leveraging AWS's extensive infrastructure, Smartling can offer enhanced automation and integration capabilities, ultimately reducing costs and improving translation quality for businesses modernizing their tech stacks. The collaboration also emphasizes the importance of co-selling strategies in driving customer success and adoption of innovative localization technologies.
