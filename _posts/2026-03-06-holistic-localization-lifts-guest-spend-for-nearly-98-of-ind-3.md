The 2026 London Book Fair will feature a lineup of notable authors and their works, with events scheduled from March 10 to March 12. K. Charles Oelfke II will be signing copies of his memoir, "Older Women Don't Giggle: Memoirs of a Renaissance Man," while Edward Barraza will present his book, "Mind Over Matter: A True Story." Additionally, Dr. Moni Kay will showcase her leadership-focused book, "3 Silent Relationship Destroyers," aimed at a global audience of industry leaders and publishers.

ReadersMagnet, a self-publishing and book marketing company, will also exhibit several titles, including "Daniela: A Story of Unbreakable Love and Resilience" by Jose Solís and "Essence of Life: Poetry & Life in Motion" by poet Jennifer Fahie. Other highlighted works include David O. Otebele's contemporary novel and Dan Bar Hava's historical conspiracy thriller, "The 36 Watchers: Book I: Fall."
