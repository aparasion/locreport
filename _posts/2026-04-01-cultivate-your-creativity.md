Anikó Pető-Mordovski, a speaker at the ELIA Together 2026 conference, emphasized the importance of nurturing creativity within the localization industry. As a co-founder of #TeamTranslator, she highlighted the value of community and collaboration, noting that creativity is essential for adapting to industry changes and enhancing job satisfaction. Her presentation drew parallels between gardening and creativity, underscoring the need for a supportive environment to cultivate innovative ideas.

In her talk, Pető-Mordovski introduced creative problem-solving methods like human-centered design thinking and the Think Bigger approach, advocating for diverse perspectives as a catalyst for creativity. She pointed out that localization service providers (LSPs) can benefit from fostering interdepartmental knowledge exchange and engaging in meaningful conversations with freelancers, ultimately leading to richer creative outcomes.

The key takeaway for localization professionals is to actively create environments that encourage diverse viewpoints and collaboration, which can significantly enhance creativity and innovation in their workflows.
