TransPerfect has reported a 7% increase in billed revenues for 2025, reaching $1.32 billion, marking its 33rd consecutive year of growth. This achievement is attributed to a mix of organic sales, strategic acquisitions—including industry leaders like Unbabel and The Mill—and a surge in customer adoption of their technology products, particularly the GlobalLink NOW AI translation portal, which now boasts over 150,000 active users.

The growth underscores significant trends in the localization and language services industry, particularly the increasing reliance on AI and automation in translation workflows. As more clients seek to streamline operations and enhance efficiency, TransPerfect's expansion into new markets and technologies positions it to meet the evolving demands of global business. The company's focus on vertical-specific solutions and AI consulting reflects a broader shift towards integrated, technology-driven language services.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the importance of embracing AI and automation to remain competitive. As demonstrated by TransPerfect, leveraging advanced technology not only drives revenue growth but also enhances client satisfaction by delivering faster, more efficient services.
