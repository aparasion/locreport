In a recent interview, Susana Peixoto reflects on her impactful tenure on the ELIA Board from 2016 to 2021, highlighting her motivations and experiences. Peixoto was inspired to join ELIA for the collaborative learning opportunities it provided, which she found invaluable for both personal and professional growth. She recalls the dedication of early board members and the successful launch of initiatives like ELIA Focus PM, emphasizing the strong team spirit during those formative years.

Peixoto also discussed the challenges faced during the COVID-19 pandemic, particularly the cancellation of a major conference in Milan. However, the board quickly adapted by transitioning to online events, which ultimately exceeded expectations and kept the organization thriving. She credits ELIA with significantly benefiting her company through networking and the implementation of new technologies.

Looking ahead, Peixoto envisions ELIA playing an even more critical role in fostering continuous learning and mentorship within the industry.
