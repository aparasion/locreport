The trend towards controlled adoption of technology, combined with a renewed focus on quality and compliance, reflects a broader shift towards operational excellence in clinical research. For localization teams, this means embracing a proactive approach to support the industry's growing need for multilingual communication that meets the demands of a globalized clinical environment.
