Smartling, Inc. has been recognized as one of Fast Company’s Most Innovative Companies for 2025 in the Business Services category, highlighting its significant impact on the localization and translation industry. The award celebrates Smartling’s advancements in AI-powered translation solutions, which help online retailers and global websites overcome language barriers with improved speed and accuracy. Their recent innovations, including the AI Translation Toolkit, have notably reduced turnaround times while maintaining high-quality outputs.

This recognition underscores the growing importance of AI and machine learning in translation management systems (TMS), as companies increasingly seek efficient and cost-effective solutions to meet their global commerce needs. Smartling's ability to integrate seamlessly with existing tech stacks positions it as a leader in the market, trusted by major brands like Pinterest and Shopify.

For localization professionals, Smartling's achievements serve as a reminder of the transformative potential of AI in enhancing translation workflows and meeting the demands of a rapidly evolving digital landscape.
