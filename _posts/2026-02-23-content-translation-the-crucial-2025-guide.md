XTM has released a comprehensive guide emphasizing the critical distinction between content translation and localization, highlighting how brands can better connect with global audiences. The article outlines that while translating content is essential for reaching wider markets, it’s localization—tailoring content to fit cultural contexts—that truly drives user engagement and conversions. Brands that overlook this may risk alienating potential customers, as evidenced by successful examples like Edgard & Cooper and Netflix, which adapt their messaging and visuals to resonate with local preferences.

For localization and language services professionals, the guide underscores the importance of aligning translation efforts with business objectives and customer expectations.
