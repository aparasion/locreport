Ivana Bojčić, CEO of BoLingo, shares her journey of transforming a kitchen-table startup into a reputable boutique language company in the latest installment of “The Voices Behind the Words: Member Spotlight.” BoLingo, which combines Bojčić's name with the Latin root for language, has grown to specialize in translations for various sectors, particularly the automotive and consumer tech industries, over its 11-year history.

Bojčić emphasizes the importance of quality and balance in her work, leading a small team of in-house translators and external collaborators. This focused approach has allowed BoLingo to build strong relationships with major global brands while maintaining a commitment to client satisfaction. The company’s unique culture includes furry office assistants, which contribute to a positive work environment.

In addition to her role at BoLingo, Bojčić expresses a desire for greater advocacy among language professionals and reflects on her passion for the arts, hinting at potential alternative career paths in television or museum work.
