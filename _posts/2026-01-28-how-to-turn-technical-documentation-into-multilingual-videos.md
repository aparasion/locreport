XTM has introduced the Video Creation Cloud (VCC), an innovative platform designed to automate the transformation of technical documentation into multilingual instructional videos. This solution addresses the common issue of outdated video content that fails to keep pace with rapidly evolving written manuals, particularly in regulated industries where compliance is critical.

The VCC streamlines the video production process by directly integrating structured content from existing documentation systems, enabling organizations to create, localize, and update videos efficiently. By leveraging AI for voiceovers and subtitles in over 80 languages, the platform reduces production time by up to 80% and localization costs by 50%. This shift from manual to automated workflows not only enhances operational efficiency but also ensures that video content remains aligned with the latest documentation, mitigating compliance risks.
