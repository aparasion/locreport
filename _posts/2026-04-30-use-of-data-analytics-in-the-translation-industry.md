A new research survey is now open, focusing on the role of data analytics in the translation industry. This initiative seeks to gather insights from language service providers (LSPs) and translation companies on how they utilize data analytics to enhance translation processes and inform decision-making. The survey, which takes approximately 10 to 15 minutes to complete, is anonymous and voluntary, ensuring confidentiality for all participants.

Understanding the impact of data analytics on translation workflows is crucial for the localization industry, as it can lead to improved efficiency, better resource allocation, and more informed strategic decisions. As data-driven approaches become increasingly prevalent, this research could illuminate trends that shape the future of translation services.

For localization professionals, participating in this survey could provide valuable insights into industry practices and contribute to a broader understanding of how data analytics can drive innovation in translation. Consider sharing your experiences and perspectives to help advance this important research.
