XTM International and Vistatec have launched a partnership to deliver an end-to-end AI globalisation service tailored for enterprises. This collaboration aims to streamline the operationalisation of AI-driven global content, enhancing governance, auditability, and expert delivery across diverse teams and markets. By integrating Vistatec’s translation services with XTM’s AI globalisation platform, organizations can achieve faster global execution while maintaining brand control and compliance.

This partnership addresses a critical need in the localization industry: the challenge of managing disparate tools and processes that can lead to inefficiencies and inconsistencies. By providing a unified operating model, enterprises can reduce duplicated efforts, improve visibility into content performance, and position globalisation as a strategic growth enabler. The service covers the entire lifecycle of global content, from intake to quality control and reporting.

For localization professionals, this collaboration represents a significant step towards operational maturity in AI-driven workflows. I encourage you to explore the full details of this partnership for insights into how it can enhance your global content strategy.
