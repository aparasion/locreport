XTM has introduced a transformative approach to localization with its concept of AI-orchestrated globalization, emphasizing the need for a shared intelligence layer across enterprise systems. While many organizations have successfully integrated AI into individual workflows, they often miss the opportunity to unify these efforts, resulting in siloed intelligence that limits scalability and efficiency. By connecting various tools and teams under one cohesive framework, businesses can leverage AI more effectively to enhance decision-making and streamline processes.

This shift from automation to orchestration is crucial for localization professionals, as it allows for consistent application of policies and contextual intelligence across diverse content types and markets. The intelligence layer not only improves workflow visibility but also ensures that AI decisions are traceable and governed appropriately, reducing risks associated with scale.
