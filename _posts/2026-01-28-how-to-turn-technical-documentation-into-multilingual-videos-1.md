XTM has introduced the Video Creation Cloud (VCC), a text-to-video automation platform designed to streamline the production of multilingual instructional videos from technical documentation. This innovation addresses a critical gap in the localization process, where video content often lags behind rapidly updated written manuals, leading to compliance risks and user confusion.

The VCC automates video creation by directly ingesting structured content from sources like DITA or XML, generating AI-driven voiceovers and subtitles in over 80 languages. This not only accelerates production times—reportedly reducing them by up to 80%—but also ensures that video content remains aligned with the latest documentation, thereby mitigating compliance issues in regulated industries. By integrating with existing localization workflows, VCC supports efficient global content management.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the potential for significant operational efficiency gains. By adopting automated video workflows, teams can enhance user experience and compliance while reducing costs and production timelines, ultimately enabling faster global releases.
